Who is your app for?
    lehigh students and local bethlehem people.
What will your app help people do?
    It will help them to find friends to go to nearby concerts and events together.
How will your app help them?

Brainstorm App Definition Statement: My app will help local people to find friends to 
    go to concerts or events they are both interested in by giving them space to post
    information about the event or concert and comment, like and indicate that they are
    going to a certain event posted by other users.
